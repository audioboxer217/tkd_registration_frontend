Tulsa Premium Outlet Mall

Distance from venue: 1.5 miles (~7 min drive)

Tulsa Premium Outlets is a shopping center located in Jenks, Oklahoma. It is open Monday through Saturday from 10:00 AM to 8:00 PM and Sunday from 11:00 AM to 6:00 PM. The center has a variety of popular designer and lifestyle brand-name outlet stores, as well as a children's play area. It is wheelchair accessible and has free parking.

You can find more information about the center on their website or by calling 918-528-8940.

Oklahoma Aquarium

Distance from venue: 1.5 miles (~7 min drive)

The Oklahoma Aquarium is located at 300 Aquarium Dr, Jenks, OK 74037. It is open Monday through Friday from 10:00 AM to 6:00 PM, Saturday from 10:00 AM to 9:00 PM, and Sunday from 10:00 AM to 6:00 PM. The aquarium has a variety of exhibits, including a walk-through tunnel where you can see sharks and stingrays, a coral reef tank, and a touch pool. The aquarium also offers educational programs and special events.

You can learn more about the aquarium on their website or by calling 918-296-3474.

Riverwalk Crossing

Distance from venue: 0.6 miles (~3 min drive)

Riverwalk Crossing is a lifestyle center located in Jenks, Oklahoma. It is open Monday through Sunday from 8:00 AM to 10:00 PM. The center has a variety of shops, restaurants, and entertainment options. It is also home to the Riverwalk Trail, a 3.5-mile trail that follows the Arkansas River.

You can find more information about the center on their website or by calling 918-518-6675.
